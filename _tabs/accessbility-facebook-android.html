Android app

On the Android app, the photo must be posted before you can add alternative text. Use a computer to add alt text to photos before posting if possible.

  • Tap on the photo.
  • Tap more_vert Options in the top right or tap and hold.
  • Tap Edit alt text.
  • Automatically generated alt text will be shown in bold starting with Image may contain:.
    Type your alt text in the box.
  • Tap Save.
