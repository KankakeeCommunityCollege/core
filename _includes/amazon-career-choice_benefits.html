Frequently Asked Questions

How much will it cost to earn my degree?
KCC is committed to making your education as affordable as possible. Tuition is currently $155 per credit hour, plus $19 in fees, for a total of $174 per credit hour. A full-time course load is 12 to 15 credit hours. Your Amazon Career Choice benefit covers $5,250 per year for full-time Amazon associates and $2,625 for part-time employees. If you decide to take more credit hours beyond your voucher allotment, your academic advisor will make you aware of scholarship opportunities and other financial aid resources.

Am I eligible to enroll?
If you've been a U.S. Amazon employee (full time or part time) for 90 days or more, and have a high school diploma or GED, you may be eligible to enroll at KCC through Amazon Career Choice. Talk to your KCC advisor to learn more.
