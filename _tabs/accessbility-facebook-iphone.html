iPhone app

On the iPhone app, the photo must be posted before you can add alternative text. Use a computer to add alt text to photos before posting if possible.

  • Tap on the photo.
  • Tap Options in the top right.
  • Tap Edit Alt Text.
  • The automatically generated alt text will be shown in bold starting with Image may contain:.
    Tap Override generated alt text.
  • Enter your alt text in the box.
  • Tap Save.
