At Kankakee Community College, we are dedicated to providing a seamless and inclusive online experience for all users. If you encounter a hurdle while using our online resources, please let us know. Your insights help us identify and resolve barriers, ensuring that every student and community member has equal access to our digital environment.

Common examples of digital barriers include:

  • Website Navigation: Difficulty using a screen reader or navigating a site using only a keyboard.
  • Inaccessible Documents: Online forms, PDFs, or spreadsheets that are not formatted for assistive technology.
  • Multimedia Gaps: Videos missing accurate closed captioning or lack of alternative text (Alt-text) for essential images.
  • Software Barriers: Third-party platforms or portals that do not meet modern accessibility standards.
