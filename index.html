Why Choose KCC?
{% include svg/save-money.html %}
Save Money

KCC costs 1/3 less than 4-year colleges and universities and offers the same great education. Plus, financial aid & scholarships can help.

{% include svg/love.html %}
Love What You Do

KCC has degree & certificate options to fit your interests. You can transfer after KCC to complete a bachelor’s degree, or enter the workforce.

{% include svg/goals.html %}
Reach Your Goals

We care about your progress and want you to succeed. KCC’s small class sizes, faculty and support services help you stay on track to graduate.

What Our Students Say

“At Kankakee Community College, I found my starting point as a first-generation college student, close to home. Now, graduating with a bachelor's in psychology from Eastern Illinois University, I cherish memories of engaging classes in Lifespan and Abnormal Psychology. Through advocacy and utilizing resources, I learned that in college, you're never alone. As a Student Trustee and part of TRIO, I grew in leadership and found support whenever I needed it.”

- Samaria Rosenthal, '22

“KCC is a great choice for me. So far, my encounters with the teachers and staff at KCC have been great! I am able to get a great education at an affordable cost. The smaller class sizes allow students to get to know the teacher and fellow classmates a lot easier than a big 200+ size class at a university. The creativity and passion many have in their area of study shows up in the classroom.”

- Mark Daniel Cross, current student
